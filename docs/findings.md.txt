# Key Findings

## Sales Performance

* Total Revenue reached £20.48M.
* Average Order Value reached £510.94.
* Over 40,000 completed orders were analysed.

## Geographic Analysis

* United Kingdom contributed 85.03% of total revenue.
* Revenue was generated across 43 countries.
* Business remains heavily dependent on the UK market.

## Product Analysis

* Top 10 products contributed 10.15% of total revenue.
* Revenue is distributed across a diversified product portfolio.
* No excessive dependency on a single product.

## Customer Analysis

* Champions contributed 47.17% of revenue.
* Loyal Customers contributed 21.90%.
* Customer retention represents a significant growth opportunity.

## Data Quality

* Over 34,000 duplicate records were identified.
* More than 19,000 cancelled transactions were analysed.
* Data cleaning significantly improved reporting reliability.
