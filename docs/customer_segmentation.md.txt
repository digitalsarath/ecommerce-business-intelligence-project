# Customer Segmentation (RFM Analysis)

## Overview

Customer segmentation was performed using the RFM framework.

RFM evaluates customers based on:

### Recency

How recently a customer made a purchase.

### Frequency

How often a customer purchases.

### Monetary

How much revenue a customer generates.

## Revenue Contribution

| Segment             | Revenue Share |
| ------------------- | ------------- |
| Champions           | 47.17%        |
| Loyal Customers     | 21.90%        |
| Others              | 19.65%        |
| At Risk             | 6.27%         |
| Potential Loyalists | 5.01%         |

## Key Insight

Champion customers represent a small portion of the customer base but contribute nearly half of total customer-attributable revenue, making customer retention a critical strategic priority.
