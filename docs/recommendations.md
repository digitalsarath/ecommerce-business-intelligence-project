# Business Recommendations

## Customer Retention

Develop loyalty programmes targeted at Champion and Loyal Customers to improve retention and maximise customer lifetime value.

## Customer Reactivation

Create campaigns focused on At Risk customers to reduce churn and encourage repeat purchases.

## Geographic Expansion

Expand marketing efforts in countries such as Germany, France, Netherlands, and EIRE to reduce dependence on the UK market.

## Product Strategy

Increase visibility of high-performing products and investigate cross-selling opportunities.

## Data Governance

Implement automated data quality checks to minimise duplicates, invalid transactions, and missing information.

## Business Intelligence

Maintain interactive dashboards to support continuous monitoring of business performance and customer behaviour.

## Future Analytics

Explore predictive analytics initiatives such as:

* Customer Churn Prediction
* Sales Forecasting
* Customer Lifetime Value Analysis
* Product Recommendation Systems
