# KPI Definitions

## Total Revenue

Total revenue generated from completed transactions.

Formula:

Revenue = Quantity × Price

Result:

£20.48M

---

## Total Orders

Number of completed orders.

Result:

40,076

---

## Average Order Value (AOV)

Formula:

Total Revenue ÷ Total Orders

Result:

£510.94

---

## UK Revenue Share

Formula:

UK Revenue ÷ Total Revenue

Result:

85.03%

---

## Top 10 Product Revenue Share

Formula:

Revenue Generated by Top 10 Products ÷ Total Revenue

Result:

10.15%

---

## Champions Revenue Share

Formula:

Champions Revenue ÷ Total Customer Revenue

Result:

47.17%
